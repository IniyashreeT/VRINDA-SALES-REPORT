Project Overview
The Vrinda Store Performance Analysis Dashboard was developed using Power BI to analyze sales performance across different channels, product categories, customer demographics, and order trends. The dashboard provides business insights that help stakeholders monitor revenue generation, customer purchasing behavior, and channel effectiveness.

Objective
The primary objective of this dashboard is to:
Track overall sales performance.
Analyze monthly sales trends.
Compare sales across different e-commerce channels.
Understand customer demographics such as gender and age groups.
Identify high-performing product categories.
Support data-driven business decision making.
Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Dashboard Components
1. Monthly Sale Trend

This line chart visualizes monthly sales performance across multiple sales channels:

Amazon
Myntra
Flipkart
Ajio
Meesho

Insights:

Tracks revenue fluctuations throughout the year.
Helps identify peak and low-performing months.
Enables comparison of channel performance over time.
2. Orders by Channels

A pie chart displaying order distribution across sales channels.

Insights:

Amazon contributes the highest share of orders.
Myntra and Flipkart are significant contributors.
Smaller platforms contribute a lower percentage of total orders.
3. Gender Split Analysis

A donut chart representing sales contribution by gender.

Insights:

Female customers contribute approximately 64% of total sales.
Male customers contribute approximately 36% of total sales.
Indicates stronger engagement from female shoppers.
4. Category Sales Analysis

A column chart comparing revenue generated by product categories.

Categories include:

Set
Kurta
Saree
Western Dress
Top
Ethnic Dress
Blouse
Bottom

Insights:

"Set" category generates the highest revenue.
Kurta and Western Dress categories also perform strongly.
Some categories contribute only a small percentage of total sales.
5. Sales Tree Analysis

A decomposition tree used to drill down total sales by category.

Insights:

Allows users to identify major revenue contributors.
Helps understand category-wise sales distribution.
Supports interactive exploration of sales performance.
6. Age Group Analysis

A demographic analysis comparing purchases by age group and gender across sales channels.

Age Groups:

Adult
Teenager
Senior

Insights:

Adult customers generate the highest revenue.
Female customers dominate most age groups.
Customer behavior differs across sales channels.

Business Benefits

This dashboard helps businesses:

Understand customer demographics.

Conclusion
The Vrinda Store Performance Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of store operations and customer purchasing behavior. Through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking, it enables management to make informed business decisions and improve overall sales performance.
